Yoga Services

Workshops & Retreats

Yoga is a multi-dimensional practice and needs to be taught as a whole and not as a part, keeping this in mind, yoga
workshops and retreats fall under a broad umbrella of four heads covering all aspects of Yoga. Workshops usually are half day or full day and residential retreats are anywhere between 2 to 5 days.

Physical aspect of Yoga
Under this section workshops encompassing the physical aspects,anatomyphysiology, movement and functional mechanics are covered. Some topics on which delivers workshops are

  • Posture correction through Asana
  • Lower back, Neck, Knee and Shoulder management
  • Functional Anatomy tutorials
  • Movement mechanics in Asana
  • Stability and Mobility
  • Breath and Prana, and so on.
